## 2. Psychographic & Behavioral Profiles

### The NUMI Customer Mindset

#### Core Motivations (What drives them)
1. **Clarity seeking** — "I want to understand WHY"
2. **Pattern recognition** — "I see patterns and want them explained"
3. **Practical application** — "I want insights I can USE"
4. **Validation** — "I want confirmation I'm on right path"
5. **Control** — "I want agency in my life direction"

#### Decision Triggers (What makes them act NOW)
1. **Life transitions** — breakup, job loss, move, birthday milestone
2. **Pain amplification** — problem becomes unbearable
3. **Social proof** — someone they trust recommends
4. **Curiosity** — intriguing content hooks them
5. **FOMO** — limited time offer or scarcity

#### Barriers to Purchase (What stops them)
1. **Skepticism** — "Is this real or just astrology?"
2. **Price sensitivity** — "Can I afford this right now?"
3. **Time commitment** — "Do I have time for another thing?"
4. **Privacy concerns** — "What will they do with my data?"
5. **Overwhelm** — "Too many options, I'll decide later"

#### Trust Builders (What overcomes skepticism)
1. **Founder story** — "Who created this and why?"
2. **Social proof** — "People like me use this"
3. **Free samples** — "Try before committing"
4. **Scientific framing** — "AI-powered" not "mystical"
5. **Money-back guarantee** — "Risk-free trial"

## 9. Risk Mitigation

### Identified Risks + Solutions

#### Risk 1: Slower User Acquisition
**Probability:** High
**Impact:** High

**Mitigation:**
- Build multiple acquisition channels (don't rely on one)
- Focus on organic/earned media (cost-effective)
- Create viral-worthy content
- Build community for word-of-mouth
- Adjust CAC target if needed

**Trigger:** Under 50 users by Week 8
**Response:** Pivot strategy, double down on organic, reassess paid

---

#### Risk 2: Low Seminar Attendance
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium

**Mitigation:**
- Make seminars shorter (30 min)
- Offer multiple time slots
- Send multiple reminders
- Create FOMO (limited spots)
- Record for those who can't attend
- Test different formats

**Trigger:** Under 20% register, under 50% show
**Response:** A/B test invitations, adjust format, add incentives

---

#### Risk 3: Low Product Sales
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium

**Mitigation:**
- Start with lower-priced items ($19-49)
- Bundle products for perceived value
- Create scarcity/urgency
- Show social proof/testimonials
- Offer payment plans
- Improve product presentation

**Trigger:** Under 10% conversion from seminar
**Response:** Survey attendees, adjust offer, test pricing

---

#### Risk 4: High Churn Rate
**Probability:** Low
**Impact:** High

**Mitigation:**
- Focus on onboarding experience
- Create habit-forming product features
- Build community around NUMI
- Regular content/app updates
- Excellent customer support
- Celebrate user milestones

**Trigger:** Over 10% monthly churn in months 2-3
**Response:** Survey churned users, improve product/experience

---

#### Risk 5: Platform Algorithm Changes
**Probability:** High
**Impact:** Medium

**Mitigation:**
- Diversify across platforms
- Build email list (owned asset)
- Create SEO-optimized content
- Develop direct referral traffic
- Don't rely on organic reach alone

**Trigger:** Sudden drop in organic reach/impressions
**Response:** Boost paid temporarily, test new content types

---

#### Risk 6: Competitive Response
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium

**Mitigation:**
- Focus on differentiation (AI, seminars, products)
- Build brand loyalty quickly
- Create unique methodology/IP
- Document customer success stories
- Stay nimble, adapt fast

**Trigger:** Major competitor launches similar features
**Response:** Emphasize differences, double down on community
