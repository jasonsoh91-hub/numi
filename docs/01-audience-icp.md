# 01 — Audience ICP: NUMI

## Audience Strategy Summary

NUMI should not target everyone equally on the first landing page.

The strongest landing page audience is the user who already has high curiosity about self-discovery, astrology, Human Design, numerology, spiritual patterns, personality frameworks, and AI-personalised guidance.

The core landing page should therefore prioritise:

1. **Primary ICP:** Spiritual Sophia — already into astrology, Human Design, numerology, and spiritual self-discovery.
2. **Secondary ICP:** Seeking Sarah — high-achieving professional seeking deeper fulfilment.
3. **Secondary ICP:** Burned Out Brenda — stressed, overwhelmed, seeking personalised relief and clarity.
4. **Content/SEO ICP:** Transitioning Tom — at a life or career crossroads, searching for direction.

---

# Primary ICP

## Persona Name

**Spiritual Sophia**

## Segment Description

Spiritual Sophia is already familiar with astrology, Human Design, numerology, tarot, energy work, or self-discovery tools. She is spiritually curious, intuitive, emotionally aware, and actively looking for deeper meaning.

She does not need to be convinced that self-discovery matters. She needs a better, more integrated way to connect what she already knows.

## Demographics

- Age: 18–35.
- Income: $40,000–$90,000.
- Background: creative, liberal arts, wellness, coaching, content creation, spirituality, design, lifestyle, personal growth, or online entrepreneurship.
- Location: global urban and digitally connected markets.
- Likely female-skewed, but not exclusive.
- Strong presence in cities and lifestyle/spiritual hubs such as Los Angeles, London, Berlin, Bali, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, New York, and similar markets.

## Psychographics

Sophia:
- Values intuition, emotional truth, self-awareness, alignment, and spiritual growth.
- Believes in synchronicity, energy, timing, signs, and inner guidance.
- Likes tools that make her feel deeply seen.
- Enjoys frameworks that explain personality, relationships, purpose, energy, and timing.
- Wants more depth than generic horoscopes.
- Enjoys beautiful, modern, premium, emotionally resonant digital experiences.
- Is attracted to language like alignment, energy, blueprint, patterns, transits, deconditioning, purpose, manifestation, soul path, and self-discovery.
- Likes spiritual tools but is sensitive to anything that feels fake, shallow, overhyped, or too commercial.

## Current Behaviours

Sophia may already use:
- Astrology apps.
- Human Design calculators.
- Numerology websites.
- Tarot readers.
- Spiritual TikTok.
- Instagram astrology pages.
- Discord communities.
- YouTube explainers.
- Pinterest moodboards.
- Journals.
- Spiritual courses.
- Self-discovery quizzes.
- Meditation or wellness apps.

## Core Pain Points

- She is juggling too many spiritual tools.
- She receives conflicting interpretations from different systems.
- She understands individual parts of her chart but struggles to synthesise them.
- Most apps feel too shallow, too generic, or too complicated.
- She wants a deeper interpretation but does not always want to pay for expensive 1:1 readings.
- She wants an integrated view across patterns, not isolated reports.
- She wants daily application, not just static personality descriptions.
- She wants a system that feels spiritual and intelligent at the same time.

## Emotional Tension

“I know there is something meaningful in all these systems, but I do not know how to connect everything into one clear picture of who I am and what I should do next.”

## Desired Outcome

Sophia wants NUMI to help her:
- Understand her full energetic and personal pattern blueprint.
- Connect numerology, astrology, Human Design, and personal growth insights.
- Receive personalised, contextual guidance.
- Turn insight into daily action.
- Feel seen, understood, and validated.
- Deepen her spiritual practice.
- Make better life, relationship, career, and emotional decisions.
- Find a single source of truth for her self-discovery journey.

## Buying Triggers

Sophia is likely to convert when:
- The free guide feels “scarily accurate.”
- She sees a post that describes her pattern perfectly.
- A friend or creator shares their Core Number.
- She is going through a relationship shift, career shift, identity shift, or emotional reset.
- She wants to understand why she repeats the same pattern.
- She wants to go deeper after receiving a quick insight.
- The product gives her a beautiful, sharable, personal result.
- The first experience feels easy, emotionally resonant, and personalised.

## Willingness to Pay

Moderate to high.

She already spends on:
- Astrology apps.
- Human Design readings.
- Tarot readings.
- Spiritual courses.
- Wellness subscriptions.
- Journals.
- Coaching.
- Community memberships.
- Digital products and guides.

She will pay if NUMI proves that it gives more integrated, accurate, and personalised value than free content.

## Main Objections

### Objection 1
“I already use astrology and Human Design apps.”

Response:
NUMI is not just another app. It connects your patterns into one intelligent system.

### Objection 2
“Can AI really understand spiritual wisdom?”

Response:
NUMI uses AI to synthesise multiple pattern systems and translate them into clear, practical guidance.

### Objection 3
“Will this be too basic for me?”

Response:
The free Pattern Code gives a simple entry point, but NUMI is designed to go deeper into thinking, love, wealth, decision, and success patterns.

### Objection 4
“I prefer human readers.”

Response:
NUMI can work as a daily companion between deeper readings, giving you reflection, prompts, and ongoing pattern guidance.

## Landing Page Messaging Angle

Primary angle:
**Stop jumping between disconnected self-discovery tools. Start with your free Pattern Code and unlock the first layer of how you think, love, earn, and decide.**

# Secondary ICP 1

## Persona Name

**Seeking Sarah**

## Segment Description

Seeking Sarah is outwardly successful but internally unfulfilled. She has achieved a lot professionally, but she senses there is a deeper layer of purpose, identity, and personal alignment that she has not fully accessed.

## Demographics

- Age: 30–45.
- Income: $100,000+.
- Education: postgraduate or highly educated.
- Location: major urban centres.
- Career: executive, founder, consultant, strategist, lawyer, finance, technology, creative leadership, marketing, or senior professional.

## Psychographics

Sarah:
- Values achievement, efficiency, optimisation, clarity, and growth.
- Wants self-discovery to feel intelligent, premium, and practical.
- May be skeptical of overly spiritual language.
- Is open to ancient wisdom if framed through modern AI and personal optimisation.
- Wants deeper fulfilment without abandoning ambition.

## Core Pain Points

- Success without fulfilment.
- Decision fatigue.
- Feeling stuck in a high-performing but misaligned life.
- Lack of deeper purpose.
- Tired of generic self-help advice.
- Wants clarity without studying complex spiritual systems.

## Desired Outcome

Sarah wants:
- A clearer sense of purpose.
- Better alignment between ambition and inner truth.
- A sophisticated self-growth tool.
- More confident decision-making.
- A way to understand herself without fluff.

## Landing Page Use

Use Sarah’s psychology in:
- Premium positioning.
- “Beyond success” messaging.
- Strategic self-discovery sections.
- Testimonials from professionals.
- Copy that makes NUMI feel intelligent and practical.

## Message Angle

**Move beyond achievement into alignment. NUMI helps high-achievers decode the patterns behind their decisions, ambition, relationships, and fulfilment.**

- She already understands self-discovery language.

- She already pays for related products.
- She feels the exact problem NUMI solves: fragmented systems and disconnected interpretations.
- She is highly reachable through visual, social, and creator-led channels.
- She is likely to share her result, creating organic growth potential.
