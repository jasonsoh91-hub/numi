# NUMI Illustration Brief

## Concept: "The Pattern Within"

A visual representation of human intelligent patterns — combining Eastern wisdom with modern technology.

---

## Option 1: Hero Section Illustration

**Theme:** Human silhouette revealing inner pattern geometry

**Visual Description:**
```
A minimalist human figure in profile view
Inside the silhouette:
  - Intricate sacred geometry patterns (flower of life, Sri Yantra inspired)
  - Interconnecting lines representing life patterns
  - Glowing nodes at key points (like a constellation)
  - Gradient colors: deep purple → indigo → soft amber

Background:
  - Clean, dark space
  - Subtle floating geometric particles
  - Light emanating from within the figure

Overall feeling: Mysterious, transformative, enlightened
```

## Typography Notes

- **Primary:** Inter (clean, modern)
- **Display:** Light tracking (letterspaced) for premium feel
- **Headings:** Light weight, not bold

---

## Designer/AI Guidelines

1. **Avoid:** Cliché meditation poses, lotus positions, typical stock "wellness" imagery
2. **Embrace:** Geometry, patterns, constellations, tech-meets-wisdom
3. **Feeling:** Exclusive, mysterious, transformational, premium
4. **Style reference:** Calm app logo, Headspace abstracts, but more sophisticated
