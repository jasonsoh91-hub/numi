## Tier Breakdown

### Level 5: The "NUMI Perfect Match" (40% of budget)
**Matches all 5 dimensions (one from each)**

```
Example: D1 + W1 + P1 + B1 + N1
= High income + Spa enthusiast + Values tranquility + Luxury purchaser + Burned out
```

**Estimated Audience Size:** 50,000 - 100,000

**Meta Ads Targeting:**
- Income: Top 10% ZIP codes
- Interests: Spa, Wellness tourism, Luxury travel, Mindfulness
- Behaviors: Luxury shoppers, Frequent travelers
- Age: 35-55

**Google Ads In-Market:**
- Spa services
- Luxury hotels
- Wellness retreats
- Stress management programs

**Budget Allocation:** $14,400/month (based on $36K contract ÷ 6 months × 40%)

---

### Level 4: The "Strong Candidate" (30% of budget)
**Matches 4 of 5 dimensions**

```
Combination examples:
- D1 + W1 + P1 + B1 (missing pain point trigger)
- D1 + W1 + P1 + N1 (missing luxury behavior)
- D2 + W2 + P2 + B2 (younger, wellness-focused)
```

**Estimated Audience Size:** 200,000 - 500,000

**Budget Allocation:** $10,800/month

---

### Level 3: The "Potential Customer" (20% of budget)
**Matches 3 of 5 dimensions**

```
Combination examples:
- Wellness enthusiasts without income qualification
- High income with luxury behavior but no wellness interest
- Urban professionals with burnout signals
```

**Estimated Audience Size:** 1,000,000 - 2,000,000

**Budget Allocation:** $7,200/month

---

### Level 2: The "Extended Reach" (8% of budget)
**Matches 2 dimensions (usually demographics + one interest)**

**Estimated Audience Size:** 5,000,000 - 10,000,000

**Budget Allocation:** $2,880/month

---

### Level 1: The "Discovery Pool" (2% of budget)
**Matches 1 dimension (broad interest targeting)**

**Purpose:** Discovery, creative testing, lookalike seeding

**Estimated Audience Size:** 20,000,000+

**Budget Allocation:** $720/month
