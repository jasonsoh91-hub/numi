# 02 — Offer Funnel: NUMI

## Funnel Objective

The landing page should convert visitors into leads and then move them toward NUMI app/product activation.

Primary conversion:
**Get My Free Pattern Insight**

Secondary conversion:
**Download The Pattern Code**

Final product bridge:
**Go deeper with NUMI.**

---

## Lead Magnet

### Name
**The Pattern Code**

### Subtitle
**Why You Think, Love, Earn & Decide the Way You Do**

### Lead Magnet Promise
Discover your Core Number and unlock your first Pattern Insight into how you think, love, earn, decide, lead, react, and repeat patterns.

### Lead Magnet Positioning
The Pattern Code is not positioned as a prediction tool. It is positioned as a mirror for inner patterns.

### Core Framing
Your number is more than a number.  
It can reveal patterns behind how you think, love, earn, decide, lead, react, and repeat cycles.

### Strategic Role
The lead magnet is the front door into NUMI.

It should:
- Create an instant self-recognition moment.
- Make self-discovery feel simple and accessible.
- Give users a reason to enter birthdate/email.
- Introduce the 9 Core Number archetypes.
- Create curiosity for deeper AI-powered interpretation inside NUMI.

# Funnel Architecture

## Stage 1 — Awareness

### Traffic Sources
- TikTok organic.
- Instagram Reels.
- Instagram carousel posts.
- Pinterest pins.
- Creator/influencer content.
- Meta ads.
- Google search.
- YouTube pre-roll or long-form content.
- Community sharing.
- Word-of-mouth through shareable Pattern results.

### Awareness Hooks
- “Your patterns are not random.”
- “Calculate your Core Number.”
- “Your birthdate may reveal how you think, love, earn, and decide.”
- “There are 9 Pattern Codes. Which one are you?”
- “One number can reveal your hidden decision pattern.”
- “This is why you keep repeating the same emotional cycle.”
- “Find your Core Number in under 60 seconds.”

## Stage 2 — Landing Page

### Page Goal
Get the visitor to request their free Pattern Insight.

### Conversion Action
User submits:
- First name.
- Email address.
- Birthdate.

Optional later:
- Birth time.
- Birth location.
- Relationship status.
- Current life focus: love, career, money, purpose, stress, transition.

### Recommended Form Fields for V1
Keep the first version simple:
1. First name.
2. Email.
3. Date of birth.

### Recommended Form CTA
**Get My Free Pattern Insight**

### Secondary CTA
**See How It Works**

## Stage 3 — Immediate Result

### Option A: Simple Version
After signup, user receives:
- The Pattern Code PDF.
- A thank-you page.
- CTA to download NUMI or unlock full pattern.

### Option B: Stronger Version
After signup, user receives:
- Their Core Number result.
- A short archetype preview.
- The Pattern Code PDF.
- CTA to unlock deeper AI interpretation in NUMI.

### Recommended V1
Use Option B if technically possible.

The best conversion moment is when the user sees their Core Number immediately. That self-recognition increases motivation to go deeper.

## Stage 4 — Thank You Page

### Thank You Page Goal
Move users from lead magnet download to NUMI activation.

### Thank You Page Message
Your first Pattern Insight is ready.

The guide reveals one layer. NUMI reveals the full map — your thinking pattern, love pattern, wealth pattern, decision pattern, and success pattern with daily AI guidance.

### Thank You Page CTAs
Primary:
**Unlock My Full Pattern in NUMI**

Secondary:
**Download The Pattern Code**

## Stage 5 — Email Nurture

### Email Sequence Goal
Convert lead magnet downloaders into product users.

### Recommended Email Sequence

#### Email 1 — Delivery
Subject: Your Pattern Code is ready  
Goal: Deliver PDF and reinforce Core Number meaning.

#### Email 2 — Self-Recognition
Subject: Your patterns are not random  
Goal: Explain how repeated patterns show up in thinking, love, money, and decisions.

#### Email 3 — Archetype Education
Subject: The 9 Pattern Codes explained  
Goal: Introduce all 9 archetypes and encourage sharing.

#### Email 4 — Product Bridge
Subject: The guide shows one layer. NUMI reveals the full map.  
Goal: Drive app activation or deeper AI insight.

#### Email 5 — Objection Handling
Subject: Is this prediction or self-understanding?  
Goal: Clarify that NUMI is not fortune telling; it is pattern interpretation and guidance.

#### Email 6 — Final CTA
Subject: Ready to unlock your full Pattern Insight?  
Goal: Drive download / signup / paid activation.

# Key Conversion Mechanics

## 1. Immediate Personalisation
The user should feel the page is about them, not about NUMI.

## 2. Low Friction
Ask for only the information needed to deliver the first result.

## 3. Self-Recognition
The first result should make the user think:
“That sounds like me.”

## 4. Curiosity Gap
The PDF should not reveal everything. It should create a desire to unlock the deeper pattern inside NUMI.

## 5. Product Bridge
Every free value touchpoint should transition naturally into:
“This is only one layer.”
