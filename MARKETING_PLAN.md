## 3. Funnel Architecture

### The NUMI Funnel Structure

```
AWARENESS → INTEREST → CONSIDERATION → CONVERSION → EXPANSION
   100%        40%          20%             5%            1%
```

### Stage 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)

**Goal:** Get discovered

**Channels:**
- Social media content (organic + paid)
- Influencer partnerships
- PR/features in wellness media
- SEO/Content marketing

**Offer:** "Discover what your birthdate reveals"

**CTA:** Download NUMI app / Get free reading

**Target CAC:** $3-5 per lead

---

### Stage 2: Interest (Middle of Funnel)

**Goal:** Capture contact info

**Experience:**
- User downloads app
- Enters name + birthdate
- Receives initial number reading

**Email Capture:**
- "Want to understand your numbers deeper?"
- Email: name + initial reading

**Target CAC:** $8-12 per email capture

---

### Stage 3: Consideration (Nurture)

**Goal:** Build trust, educate

**Sequence:**
1. **Email 1 (Day 0):** "Your NUMI reading + what it means"
2. **Email 2 (Day 2):** "How your numbers affect your career"
3. **Email 3 (Day 4):** "Free seminar invitation"
4. **Email 4 (Day 6):** "Last chance to register"

**Seminar:**
- 45-minute live session
- Explains NUMI system
- Shows real examples
- Soft introduces products
- Q&A

**Target CAC:** $15-20 per seminar registrant

---

### Stage 4: Conversion (First Purchase)

**Goal:** Convert to paying customer

**Options:**
1. **App Subscription** - $9.99/month or $79/year
2. **Product Purchase** - $29-149

**Post-Seminar Offer:**
- Special pricing for 48 hours
- Bundles: App + Product
- Scarcity: Limited spots

**Target CAC:** $20-30 per first purchase

---

### Stage 5: Expansion (Upsell)

**Goal:** Maximize LTV

**Upsell Sequence:**
1. Month 1: App subscription → Product offer
2. Month 2: Single product → Product bundle
3. Month 3: Bundle → Premium membership
4. Month 6: Customer → Affiliate/Partner

---

## 4. User Journey Maps

### Journey A: The Quick Converter (30% of users)

```
Day 0: Sees ad → Downloads app → Gets reading
Day 1: Receives email → Skims → Clicks product link
Day 3: Makes first purchase ($29-49)
Day 30: Receives nurture email → Buys again
```

**Characteristics:** Impulse buyer, price-sensitive, lower LTV ($75-100)

---

### Journey B: The Seminar Attendee (40% of users)

```
Day 0: Sees ad → Downloads app → Gets reading
Day 1: Receives email → Reads thoroughly
Day 4: Registers for seminar
Day 7: Attends seminar → Asks questions
Day 8: Follow-up email → Makes first purchase ($49-99)
Day 30: Receives nurture → Buys app subscription
Day 60: Another product purchase
```

**Characteristics:** Relationship-driven, wants education, higher LTV ($150-250)

---

### Journey C: The Loyal Member (20% of users)

```
Day 0: Organic discovery → Downloads app
Day 1: Joins email list
Day 7: Attends seminar
Day 8: Purchases product bundle ($99)
Day 30: Subscribes to app ($9.99/mo)
Day 90: Upgrades to premium
Day 180: Becomes affiliate
```

**Characteristics:** Fully invested, high LTV ($300-500), refers others

---

### Journey D: The Slow Nurture (10% of users)

```
Month 1: Downloads app, doesn't engage
Month 2: Continues receiving emails
Month 3: Attends seminar
Month 4: First purchase
```

**Characteristics:** Skeptical, needs trust, medium LTV ($100-150)

## 5. Upsell Flow

### The NUMI Upsell Ladder

```
FREE → LOW TICKET → SUBSCRIPTION → MID TICKET → HIGH TICKET → PARTNER
```

### Detailed Upsell Sequence

#### Step 1: Free (The Hook)
- App download
- Initial number reading
- Email capture

**Value:** Creates contact, demonstrates value

---

#### Step 2: Low Ticket ($9-29) - The Impulse Buy
**Timing:** Immediately after reading or 24-48 hours later

**Offers:**
- "Your Personal Number Guide" (PDF) - $9
- "7-Day NUMI Journey" (email course) - $19
- "Mini Energy Object" - $29

**Conversion Goal:** 15-20% of free users

**Purpose:** Overcome purchase resistance, get first payment method

---

#### Step 3: Subscription ($9.99/month or $79/year) - The Recurring
**Timing:** Post-seminar or 7 days after low ticket

**Offer:**
- App premium features
- Weekly insights
- Community access
- Monthly live Q&A

**Conversion Goal:** 25-30% of low ticket buyers

**Purpose:** Create predictable revenue

---

#### Step 4: Mid Ticket ($49-149) - The Core Product
**Timing:** Post-seminar or 14-30 days in

**Offers:**
- "NUMI Energy Kit" - $49
- "Personalized Number Jewelry" - $79
- "Home Alignment Set" - $99
- "Complete NUMI System" (bundle) - $149

**Conversion Goal:** 20-25% of subscribers

**Purpose:** Main profit center

---

#### Step 5: High Ticket ($299-997) - The Premium
**Timing:** 60-90 days in, for highly engaged users

**Offers:**
- "1-on-1 NUMI Reading" - $299
- "NUMI Certification Program" - $497
- "Exclusive Retreat Access" - $997

**Conversion Goal:** 5-10% of mid-ticket buyers

**Purpose:** Maximize LTV from top customers

---

#### Step 6: Partner/Affiliate - The Multiplier
**Timing:** 90+ days, for satisfied customers

**Offer:**
- Affiliate program (20% commission)
- Partner program (wholesale pricing)
- Certification (teach NUMI)

**Conversion Goal:** 10-15% of high-ticket buyers

**Purpose:** Turn customers into acquisition channels

---

### Downsell Options (If No Purchase)

| Rejection | Downsell Offer | Timing |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| Didn't buy low ticket | Free 7-day email course | Immediately |
| Didn't subscribe | $9 one-time PDF | 3 days later |
| Didn't buy mid ticket | $19 mini product | 7 days later |
| Didn't attend seminar | Recording + transcript | Post-seminar |
