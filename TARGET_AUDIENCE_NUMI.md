## Ideal Customer Profile (Level 5)

**Name:** "Seeking Sarah" or "Transitioning Tom"

**Demographics:**
- Age: 32-48
- Income: $100K+
- Education: College graduate
- Location: Major metro (SF, LA, NYC, Seattle)

**Psychographics:**
- Feels "something is missing" despite external success
- Curious about astrology, Human Design, or Eastern philosophy
- Willing to invest in personal growth
- Tech-comfortable, uses apps daily
- Seeking deeper answers, not surface-level fixes

**Pain Points:**
- "I've achieved success but feel unfulfilled"
- "I keep repeating the same patterns in relationships/career"
- "I want to understand my purpose"
- "I'm at a crossroads and need clarity"

**Behaviors:**
- Pays for Calm, Headspace, or similar
- Has attended a workshop or retreat
- Follows astrology or Human Design accounts
- Reads self-help books
- Willing to pay $2,999-₹7,999/month for the right solution

**Why NUMI Appeals:**
- Combines ancient wisdom with modern technology
- Structured system (not random)
- Community of like-minded people
- Potential for business/partnership opportunity
- Exclusive, not mass-market
